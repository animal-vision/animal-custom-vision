User Guide

How to Use the Application

1. Stable Internet Connection
  • Before Proceeding, ensure you have a reliable internet connection
2. Uploading an Image
  • To access the image upload page, select “Go to Upload” from the dashboard or select “Upload & Analyse” in the sidebar.
  • - On a Desktop Device: click the “Choose File” button, select the image file you wish to upload, and then click   “Open.”
  • - On a Mobile Device: Tap the “Choose File” button, select one of the options listed. You can either take a photo,   upload an image from your photo library, or select a file. After making your selection, tap “Done.”
  • Once the upload is complete, an image preview will be displayed.
3. Analysing an Image
  • Click or tap the “Analyse” button located below the image preview
  • Wait while the system processes the image.
  • After processing, view the analysis results along with a confidence percentage for each prediction.
4. Viewing other pages
  • You can easily access other pages such as the dashboard, user guide, and FAQ page by clicking the corresponding links in the sidebar.
5. Tips for Optimal Use
  • To achieve the best analysis results, use high-quality images.
  • Ensure that your images are in supported formats to avoid any upload errors.
