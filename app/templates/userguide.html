User Guide

How to Use the Application

1. Stable Internet Connection
  • Before proceeding, ensure you have a reliable internet connection.
2. Uploading an Image
  • Navigate to the image upload page via “Go to Upload” on the dashboard or “Upload & Analyse” in the sidebar.
    • Desktop

    Click the “Choose File” button.

    Select your image.

    Click “Open.”

    Mobile

    Tap “Choose File.”

    Select from your photo library, take a new photo, or choose a file.

    Tap “Done” to confirm.

  • Once uploaded, a preview of the image will be displayed.
3. Analysing an Image
  • Click or tap the “Analyse” button below the image preview.
  • Wait while the system processes the image.
  • Once complete, view the analysis results along with confidence percentages for each prediction.
4. Viewing Other Pages
  • Use the sidebar to easily access other pages such as the dashboard, user guide, and FAQ page.
5. Tips for Optimal Use
  • Use high-quality images for the best analysis results.
  • Ensure your images are in supported formats to prevent upload errors.
