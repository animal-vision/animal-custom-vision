Frequently Asked Questions

To upload an image, navigate to the “Upload & Analyse” page and click the “Choose File” button. On desktop devices, select a valid image file and click “Open.” For mobile devices, you can upload a photo from your photo library, choose a file, or take a photo or video. Once selected, tap “Done” to complete the upload.

Ensure you are on the “Upload & Analyse” page and that you have uploaded an image, which should be visible in the preview. Below the image preview, click the “Analyse” button to view the predictions.

Common reasons for upload issues include:

  • The image format is not supported. The system accepts jpg, png, bmp and gif formats.
  • The file size exceeds the limit. Files larger than 6MB are not accepted, so consider compressing the image.
  • An unstable internet connection. Please check your network and try again.

To improve accuracy, use high-resolution images, avoiding blurry or low-quality ones. Ensure the image meets the system requirements outlined in the user guide.
